Technical Approach

Cloud Infrastructure: To ensure the platform scales smoothly, cloud technologies will be leveraged, allowing for cost-effective development using Laravel, Python, and React.js.
Inclusivity and Sorting: The platform will sort jobs by age, gender, and reservation categories to promote inclusivity in the workforce, focusing on rural and non-graduate workers.
